# Car Website Project — User Stories

---

## User Personas

The platform serves three distinct user types. Every user story is written from the perspective of one of these personas.

---

### 👤 Public User

A visitor who has not registered or logged in. They can browse the homepage, view car listings, use the search page, read the About page, and submit the contact form. They cannot access personalized features, save items, or perform any write operations. Their primary goal is to discover and explore cars.

---

### 🔐 Registered User

A visitor who has created an account and is currently logged in. They inherit all capabilities of the public user and additionally gain access to protected features such as managing a personal wishlist. Their primary goal is to track cars they are interested in and engage more deeply with the platform.

---

### 🛡️ Admin

A privileged user with full backend access. Admins can log in to a dedicated admin panel where they can create, read, update, and delete car records, manage categories, and update pricing. Admin routes are protected server-side and are not accessible to public or registered users. Their primary goal is to keep the car catalogue accurate and up to date.
