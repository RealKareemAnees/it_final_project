Project Goals

Our main objective is to provide a seamless, high-end digital showroom for luxury cars. Bergo is designed to spotlight premium vehicles through an immersive and distraction-free user experience, merging elegant aesthetics with peak performance.

Our Capabilities

  • Immersive 3D Rendering: High-performance GLB model previews.
  • Responsive Design: A consistent high-end feel across desktop, tablet, and mobile.
  • Smart Architecture: Modular architecture ensuring speed and reliability.

Our Team & Project Background

We are a dedicated group of software engineering and computer science students collaborating to bring the Bergo digital showroom to life. Our team combines various skills across frontend development, backend architecture, and quality assurance to deliver a seamless web experience. This project reflects our commitment to applying modern web technologies, structured problem-solving, and building high-performance, user-centric applications.
